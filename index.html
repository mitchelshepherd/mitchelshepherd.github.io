About

I’m a proud Husband and Father. I have been married for 4 years to my best friend. When I get spare time from family and work, you will find me in the outdoors either hiking or rock climbing. I have been an avid outdoorsman since the day I could walk. Some other hobbies I enjoy are scuba diving, surfing and motorcycle riding. I am a U.S. Army Veteran who served for six years. I had the amazing opportunity to serve with U.S. Space command and U.S Cyber command. The jobs and experiences I’ve had in my life have helped me advance my abilities in communications, problem solving and critical thinking. I have always made it a pillar in my life to further my education. After I graduated high school, I went on to get several certificates in strategic Intelligence, counterintelligence and cyber warfare. I then decided to further my education and I enrolled in Western Governors University in their BS Cyber Security and Information Assurance program. I’m currently enrolled and projected to graduate this year. After graduating I intend to move forward to getting my master’s and eventually a doctorates degree. In 5-10 years from now I see myself as an IT and Cyber security professional who is striving to make a difference by serving others. I intend to be working for a company that I enjoy. I intend to stick with them for the long haul and show dedication to that organization. I would love to utilize what I have been taught and my experiences to help those around me and to help an organization grow.
